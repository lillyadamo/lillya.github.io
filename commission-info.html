Portraits (Busts, waists up) - no background $40 B/W ~$65 full color

Portraits (Full body) -no background ~$50 B/W ~$75 full color

Additional elements such as: plants, animals, backgrounds etc. will be factored into the price,
which will be negotiated in the first few emails.

Commission process:

  1. I, the artist, will receive an email from you and if I accept the commission work, we will begin the commission process.
  2. Details are exchanged, (subjects, palettes, composition, time etc.)
  3. I get paid 100% of the full payment through PayPal
  4. I will begin the sketch then send to the client for feedback and approval.
    1. If the sketch needs revision, I will revise
    2. I get the final sketch approved
